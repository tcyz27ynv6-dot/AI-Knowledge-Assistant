# Engineering FAQ

Q: What is FloCard?

A: FloCard is a digital business card platform developed by 366Pi Technologies.

Q: Why use digital business cards?

A: They are sustainable, easy to share, and easier to update than paper cards.

Q: What database is used in this project?

A: ChromaDB is used as the vector database.

Q: Which LLM is used?

A: Llama 3.1 running through Ollama.
